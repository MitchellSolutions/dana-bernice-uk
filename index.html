About Dana Bernice

She creates the portrait, designs the jewellery, designs the dress, then brings them to life.

Dana Bernice is a UK-based artist, fashion designer, and jeweller.
Every face in her portraits is invented from scratch: no models, no references, no photographs. Her pencil creates people who do not exist, then brings them to life.

Her signature DB fashion and gold jewellery collections are drawn by the same hand that draws her portraits. Three disciplines. One artist. One signature.

She creates the portrait, designs the jewellery, designs the dress, then brings them to life. There is nobody else doing all three. Collector, UK
